**Setup:** Loom with screen share of `index.html` open in Chrome, camera bubble bottom-right. Inter font, dark mode, fullscreen browser.
**Thumbnail:** frame on the Hook Lab leaderboard with the top 28/30 row visible.

## RULES FOR THE RECORD

1. Record 3 takes minimum. The first is always bad. Pick the one where your voice drops at the end of the "$240K" line.
2. No stammering on numbers. Rehearse "two million, four thousand, two hundred forty thousand" out loud twice before take one.
3. No hedges: kill "kind of," "sort of," "hopefully," "I guess."
4. No smiling on the open. Smile at 2:20 on "the tool is yours" if at all.
5. Frame the camera bubble in the bottom-right. Do NOT use a big face-cam layout.
6. Mouse movements should be deliberate — no circling, no scrolling up-and-down. Move, stop, click.
7. Title the Loom: **"Dealroom Post Engine — Saif · application"**. Thumbnail: Hook Lab leaderboard frame.
